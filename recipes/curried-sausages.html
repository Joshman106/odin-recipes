Curried Sausages

Description

Curried Sausages are one of my favourite recipes. My father introduced me to them while I was young and I have loved them ever since!

Ingrediants

  • 8 Susages
  • 4 Potatos
  • 1 Brown Onion
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Boil Sausages until boiling
  2. Peel Sausages skin and chop into pieces
  3. Peel, Chop, and Steam potatos
  4. Brown onion in oil and return chopped sausages
