Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, also known as Garfields favourite dish contains layers of pasta and beschamel sauce with meat and vegetables.

Ingrediants

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Bitta Mince
  • Bitta Vegetables
  • Beschamel

Steps

  1. Lay down sheet of lasagna
  2. Add meat and sauce
  3. Sprinkle Vegetables
  4. Repeat Until dish is full
